Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 22, 2025

Welcome to Random Fact Atlas. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website (randomfactatlas.com).

1. Information We Collect

We collect very limited information to provide you with a fun and interactive experience:

  • Location Data (Voluntary): When you click on the map, we send the coordinates (latitude/longitude) of that specific click to our backend to generate a fact. We do not track your real-time physical location unless you explicitly grant browser permission to "Find My Location."
  • Usage Data: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our site (e.g., which cities are most popular).
  • Cookies: We and our third-party vendors use cookies to serve ads and analyze traffic.

2. How We Use Third-Party Services

We utilize trusted third-party services to power this website. Each has its own privacy practices:

A. Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google uses cookies (specifically the DoubleClick cookie) to serve ads based on your visit to this site and other sites on the Internet.

  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

B. Mapbox

Our interactive 3D globe is powered by Mapbox. Please refer to Mapbox's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle data.

C. Google Gemini (AI)

When you request a fact, the city name and coordinates are sent to Google's Gemini AI API to generate the text. We do not send personal identifiers to the AI model.

3. Cookies

We use cookies for functionality (like remembering your theme preference) and for Google Analytics and AdSense. You can control cookie behavior in your browser settings.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
