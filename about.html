About Random Fact Atlas

Random Fact Atlas is an educational resource designed to make geography and history accessible through interactive technology. By combining high-resolution 3D mapping from Mapbox with the generative capabilities of Google's Gemini AI, we provide a unique window into the world.

Our Mission

We believe that every location on Earth has a story. Traditional maps show you where places are, but they rarely tell you what happened there. Our goal is to bridge that gap, providing instant, verified, and interesting trivia about any coordinate on the globe.

How It Works

When you click a location on our globe, our system performs a reverse-geocoding lookup to identify the nearest city or landmark. This data is then processed by our AI engine to retrieve historical facts, cultural insights, or geographical anomalies specific to that region.
